DESIGN QUALITY CONTROL:

The website must feel visually excellent, but the design must serve credibility and conversion.

The design should feel:
- premium
- original
- futuristic but believable
- polished
- editorially clean
- product-grade
- mobile-smooth
- high-trust

The design should not feel:
- template-like
- generic agency-like
- overloaded with glowing effects
- gimmicky
- crypto/gaming styled
- overly cyberpunk
- cluttered
- fake-enterprise
- concept-art only

Visual execution rules:
- Use layered depth, restraint, hierarchy, and spacing
- Use premium typography scale and readable contrast
- Use motion to guide attention, not to show off
- Use cinematic imagery strategically, not everywhere
- Maintain strong negative space and premium pacing
- Keep the interface calm, modern, and serious
- Build a visual system that supports healthcare trust, operational software credibility, and business seriousness
